Ibyerekeye KEDI Money Network

KEDI Money Network ni urubuga rw’ikoranabuhanga rwubakiye ku muco w’imikoranire mu matsinda y’ikimina. Rwubatswe mu rwego rwo gufasha abantu kwizigamira, kubikuza, gusaba inguzanyo ndetse no gukurikirana inyungu binyuze mu buryo bwa tree plan.

Urubuga rugufasha gufungura konti, ukayikoresha ukoresheje mobile money cyangwa banking, ukabona uko wunguka bitewe n’abagiye binjira unyuzeho (referrals).

KEDI igufasha kandi kubona raporo zose z’ibikorwa byawe, aho wabikije, inguzanyo wasabye, inyungu wakiriye, ndetse no kureba uko system yose imeze ukoresheje tree plan view.

Intego nyamukuru ni uguteza imbere uburyo bwo kwizigamira no gutanga inguzanyo hagati y’abakoresha binyuze mu mucyo, umutekano n’ubunyamwuga.
